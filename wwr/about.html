History

Nestled in the Sierra Nevada, the White Water Rafting Company sprang from the Rivera family’s passion for the river’s wild rapids. They turned their love into a thriving business, inviting others to experience the river’s exhilarating embrace, creating a legacy that flowed as fiercely as the waters they adored.

Experience the thrill of water rafting! Feel the adrenaline rush as you navigate through roaring rapids, surrounded by stunning natural scenery. Every paddle brings a new challenge and adventure. It’s not just a sport, it’s a heart-pounding, unforgettable journey on the wild side of water!
