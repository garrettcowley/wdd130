Overview

Purpose

We at Rafting Company have a passion for adventure and fun! Our mission is to share that passion with you! Join us in thrilling white water expeditions or soothing calm water forays. Either way, you'll be able to find sanctuary in the warm embrace of nature. Our goal is to reach out to as many people as we can through the use of the internet and share our passion with them. We hope to build a client base to sustain and expand operations in the future.

Audience

  1. Who is our audience? Our target audience is able bodied late teens and adults.
  2. What do they want? They will want nature experience coupled with the exciting acitivity of rafting.
  3. How will they access this site? We will design this site to be friendly to all internet capable devices.
